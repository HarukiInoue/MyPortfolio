# Portfolio Goals

## Primary Goal

Make photographs feel like the main subject on every viewport.

## Secondary Goals

- Create an easy browsing rhythm for mixed portrait and landscape images.
- Keep navigation clear and minimal.
- Use typography as quiet context, not visual competition.
- Keep implementation simple enough for iterative design changes.
- Add interaction that makes the photographic opening feel immersive and then resolves into the grid.
- Give long-gallery browsing a quiet sense of position.

## Success Criteria

- The first viewport is clearly photographic.
- Gallery images are large enough to inspect.
- Spacing feels intentional on desktop and mobile.
- Captions support the images without dominating them.
- The design can be improved in small, reversible steps.
- Scroll interaction clarifies the move from large image to structured portfolio.
- Current-photo orientation updates while browsing works.
